TRUE LOVE - ft. TIM GODFREY

True Love is a song about God's endless and unfailing love. We live in a world where the term "love" has been abused. True Love is selfless, enduring, forgiving and in Christ we find the true meaning of Love.

This song was written out of a personal experience of the unwavering love of God. Enjoy his unconditional love as you dance along.
